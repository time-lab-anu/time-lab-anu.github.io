Anomaly Detection

Video Anomaly Detection (VAD) aims to automatically identify unusual occurrences in videos, enabling various applications in surveillance and monitoring.

Video/Image Processing

This focuses on motions/dynamics distillation from video data towards more lightweight scene understanding models (i.e., for action recognition, anomaly detection, etc.). Distilled motions from video data at different granular levels helps with downstream video processing tasks.

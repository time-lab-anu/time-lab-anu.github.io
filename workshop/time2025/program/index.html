Awards

To celebrate exceptional research and inspire excellence, TIME 2025 will proudly present two distinguished awards:

  • TIME Transformative Award: Honoring groundbreaking contributions that redefine the boundaries of knowledge and practice, showcasing transformative innovation and impact in the field of multi-faceted evaluation.
  • TIME Insight Award: Recognizing research that offers profound insights and rigor, advancing the understanding of complex challenges and solutions within the domain.

Winners of these prestigious awards will receive formal recognition during the workshop, offering them an unparalleled opportunity to showcase their work to a distinguished audience of experts, scholars, and practitioners. These awards not only highlight exemplary research but also inspire the community to strive for innovation and excellence.

Take advantage of this chance to have your work celebrated on a global stage and make a lasting impact in the field!
